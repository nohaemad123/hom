Welcome to HOM Egypt

In 2010, H.O.M started out as a small Business, For over 5 years H.O.M has been meticulous about quality, Comfort, and the advancement of Air Treatment.

H.O.M has dedicated its efforts in ensuring that our customers always have complete control over their environment and over all of the services/products that they receive from us, and with our continual developments, Electromechanical Projects all over Egypt never be the same again.

H.O.M will always function and perform in accordance to international standards. Customer satisfaction is our number one priority and we promise to continue providing them with unique and individual solutions.

At H.O.M, we have been developing our products & services since 2010.

By basing ourselves on the features demanded by larger companies, we have created innovative, user-friendly applications that are now available to small and medium- sized companies.

Our expertise in Constructing and Maintenance in Electromechanical Projects provided us with the knowledge to develop solutions that answer the needs of today's clients. These solutions allow you to:

  • Increase productivity
  • Reduce the operating costs
  • Improve Work Condition

At H.O.M we take the future growth of our business very seriously. We are continually introducing new products to our range and are always striving to improve ourselves and the community we live in.

HOM Egypt Vision

To evolve a brand with essence and to be the recognized performance leader of the Electromechanical Projects. Being a performance leader means we will achieve operational excellence, industry-leading customer satisfaction and superior financial performance.

HOM Egypt Mission

Provide the highest level of customer service and the finest quality products and technical services in the highly competitive Electromechanical service industry in Egypt.

HOM Egypt Values

Our goal for Cruise is to be the most respected Electromechanical company through innovation and encouraging the use of efficient Electromechanical Systems in an honest, trustworthy, reliable and an environmentally sound manner.

1- Corporate Objectives:

  • Profitability: Net profit 15% of Sales yearly
  • Volume: Market Share 30% of Petroleum & Pharmaceutical and Construction Sectors at the end 2016
  • Stability: Variance in annual sales volume trended to up down
  • Nonfinancial: Improved H.O.M image

2- Marketing Objectives:

  • Create Brand image at the Electromechanical Projects
  • Build Reputation among the Electromechanical
  • Establish long-term business Relationship with our customer
